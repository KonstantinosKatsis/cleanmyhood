# CleanMyHood

## About the App

Hood Cleaning Organizer is a web app designed to organize and promote cleaning efforts across Greece.
The platform allows users to report places that need cleaning by uploading an image and sharing their location (latitude and longitude).

How it Works

-   Create a Cleaning Request: Users can upload a photo of the area that needs cleaning, along with their location details.

-   Admin Approval: Submitted cleaning requests require admin approval before they appear publicly on the app.

-   Discover Cleaning Events: Approved cleaning spots ("hoods") are showcased on the homepage.

-   Search by Location: Users can search for nearby cleaning events by providing their location.

-   Mark a Hood as Cleaned: After completing a cleanup, users can upload a "cleaned" photo. Once verified by an admin, the status of the cleaning spot will be updated to "done."

The app aims to make it easy for communities to identify, organize, and celebrate the cleaning of local areas!
